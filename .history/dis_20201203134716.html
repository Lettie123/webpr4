&Minorities

Minorities are especially vulnerable to statelessness.

Minorities make up a large portion of the world’s stateless population. This is due in part to indirect discrimination: as marginalized communities, they are especially likely to struggle with lack of resources and limited access to official systems of registration

Minorities are disadvantaged ethnic, national, religious, linguistic or cultural groups who are smaller in number than the rest of the population
