We are from the Roma community, which is also called Gypsies. All of our family members are stateless.

There are a lot of reasons for Romanies to be stateless. We cannot go anywhere; we do not have any documents.

Roma is the largest stateless minority in Macedonian. And we are not the only minority group who remain stateless in the world.
